Community Guidelines

Maintain absolute sobriety (zero tolerance for drugs or alcohol).

Submit to random drug and alcohol testing.

Attend mandatory house meetings.

Actively participate in a recovery program (AA, NA, SMART Recovery, etc.).

Maintain employment, enroll in school, or actively volunteer.

Complete assigned daily/weekly household chores.

Adhere to the house curfew.

Respect all housemates and staff members.

Keep personal spaces and common areas clean and tidy.

No overnight guests allowed.

These rules are designed to protect the recovery environment of our home. By joining Horizon House, you agree to abide by these guidelines to support your own recovery and that of your housemates.
