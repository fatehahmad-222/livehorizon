What We Offer

More than a place to sleep.
A place to rebuild.

Horizon House is built on three foundations — structure, community, and accountability. Each resident walks a path toward independence while never walking it alone.

01

Safe Housing

Fully furnished, clean, and drug-free private and shared rooms in a welcoming home environment. All utilities included.

02

Structure & Accountability

House meetings, curfews, and regular drug testing create a framework that supports sobriety — especially in the critical early months.

03

Community

Surround yourself with people who understand the journey. Peer support, shared meals, and mutual accountability are the heartbeat of Horizon House.

04

12-Step Integration

Regular meeting attendance is encouraged. We work alongside AA, NA, and other peer recovery programs to support your chosen path.

05

Life Skills

From budgeting and job searching to cooking and time management — residents rebuild the daily rhythms that sustain long-term independence.

06

Aftercare Connection

We connect residents with outpatient treatment, counseling, employment resources, and community services to build a lasting support network.

Our Belief

Every person who walks through our door deserves dignity, respect, and a genuine opportunity to rebuild their life on solid ground.

Our Approach

We hold residents to high standards because we hold high belief in their potential. Structure is not punishment — it is the container in which transformation happens.

Our Promise

A safe, substance-free home where you are known, supported, and challenged to become the person you are capable of being.

Who We Welcome

Horizon House is for those ready to do the work.

🏥

Post-Treatment

Transitioning from inpatient rehab, detox, or a residential program and need a structured next step before returning to independent living.

⚖️

Court-Referred

Referred by a court, probation officer, or drug program. We work alongside case managers and accept court-ordered placements.

🔄

Returning to Recovery

Experienced a relapse and ready to recommit. No judgment — only support for the next chapter of your recovery journey.

🏠

Unstable Home Environment

Your current living situation puts your sobriety at risk. Horizon House offers a stable, trigger-free alternative.
